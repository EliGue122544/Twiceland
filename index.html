About Twice

Twice is a kpop girl group band that is consist of 9 members from oldest to youngest Im Nayeon, Yoo Jungyeon, Hirai Momo, Minatazaki Sana, Park Jihyo, Myoui Mina, Kim Dahyun, Son Chaeyoung and Chou Tzuyu. They debuted on October 20, 2015 and in the past 6 years Twice won many awards, sold tours and hundreds of albums. Twice also has many anti-fans that threaten them but even though they gone through many ups and downs. Twice still continue to make songs and perform the best they got! And I hope many people will listen to there songs.
